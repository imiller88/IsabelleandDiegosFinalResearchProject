In the third pair assignment you will gather web based data from at least two sources, merge the data sets, conduct basic descriptive and inferential statistics on the data to address a relevant research question and briefly describe the results including with dynamically generated tables and figures. Students are encouraged to access data and perform statistical analyses with an eye to answering questions relevant for their Collaborative Research Project. Deadline 19 April, the write up should be 1,500 words maximum and use literate programming, 10% of final grade.
